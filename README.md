This is a government welfare portal  Here's what's included:
Homepage features:

Official header with emblem, Hindi/English branding, and nav bar
Live news ticker with scheme announcements
Hero section with 4 live statistics (beneficiaries, disbursed amount, schemes, DBT rate)
15 service icons (Housing, Education, Health, Pension, Disability, Agriculture, etc.)
6 featured scheme cards (PM Awas, Ayushman Bharat, PM KISAN, MUDRA, etc.)
12 quick-access links (Status Tracker, Forms, Grievance, DigiLocker, etc.)
Helpline bar with toll-free numbers and SMS service
Full footer with ministry branding

Application Form (6 Steps):

Registration — Mobile OTP + Email OTP verification with animated 6-box OTP input, password creation
Personal Details — Aadhaar, DOB, gender, caste, income, address, state/PIN
Education & Employment — Dynamic add/remove qualification entries, employment status
Bank & Family Details — DBT bank details (IFSC, account), family/disability info
Document Upload — 8 drag-and-drop upload zones (Aadhaar, photo, income proof, caste cert, etc.)
Review & Submit — Declaration checkboxes, summary, auto-generated reference number

The design uses a deep navy/gold government color scheme with Noto Serif and IBM Plex Sans fonts for an authentic, high-quality feel.
